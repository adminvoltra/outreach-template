Trust questions

Everything we’re asked, answered.

Why isn’t the mission a registered 501(c)(3) yet?

It will be. We chose to start the work first and formalize the legal structure carefully rather than rush a filing. Registration takes time and we don’t want to publish anything that isn’t verifiable. That paperwork is in motion.

Who oversees the spending today?

Spending is currently overseen by the founder using documented internal procedures and platform-level transaction records (Stripe, PayPal, Spotfund). A formal board and external review process are being established.

Is my contribution tax-deductible?

Not until our IRS 501(c)(3) status is finalized and published here. We don’t imply otherwise on any receipt or page.

Why publish unaudited numbers?

Because silence is worse. We’d rather show our internal totals with the disclaimer that they’re unaudited than wait until everything is perfect to say anything at all.

How often will reports be posted?

Quarterly recaps, plus a campaign-level recap whenever we run a major push.
