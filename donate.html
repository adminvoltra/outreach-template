Donation FAQ

Honest answers about giving.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Not at this time. Federal tax-exempt status is in development and is not yet confirmed. We do not claim 501(c)(3) status until that registration is finalized and verifiable. Once confirmed, we will publish our EIN here and update receipt language.

How will my contribution be used?

Direct outreach: meals, hygiene kits, clothing, transportation, and emergency support. A small portion covers operating costs (payment processing, basic tools, supplies). Quarterly recaps published on the transparency page show the breakdown.

How do I cancel monthly giving?

You can cancel any time from the receipt email confirmation, or by emailing us. There’s no penalty, no questions asked.

Can I give in-kind (clothing, food, supplies)?

Yes. Reach out via the contact page and we’ll coordinate drop-off or pickup for outreach-ready items.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes — emailed immediately after your gift. Keep it for your records.
