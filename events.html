First time?

What to expect on outreach day.

You'll meet the team at the location, get a quick brief, and pair up with someone experienced. We rotate stations — meals, kits, prayer, and conversation — so everyone finds a place to plug in.

Wear comfortable shoes. Bring water. No experience needed — just a willingness to be present with people.
