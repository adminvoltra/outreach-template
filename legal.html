Your privacy, rights, and our policies.

Plain-language summaries of how we handle your data, donations, products, and access to this site.

Privacy policy

We collect only the information needed to do what you've asked us to do — process a contribution, register you for an event, ship an order, send you a newsletter, or respond to a message. We do not sell, rent, or share donor or supporter data with third parties for marketing purposes.

What we collect: name, email, mailing address (when relevant), donation amount and method, event registration details, order details, and basic site analytics (page views, referral source, device type).

Tools we use: Stripe and PayPal for payments, an email-marketing platform for our newsletter, Google Analytics for traffic insight, and managed hosting for the site itself. Each of these has its own privacy policy.

Your rights: you can request a copy of your data, ask us to update it, or ask us to delete it at any time. Reach out via the contact page.

Terms of use

By using this site you agree to use it lawfully and respectfully. Content on the site (text, images, video, logos) is owned by {{ORG_NAME}} or its founder unless otherwise credited. Don't copy or redistribute without permission.

The site is provided as-is. We work hard to keep information accurate but make no warranty as to completeness — particularly around legal status while formal nonprofit registration is in progress.

Donation policy

Important: Federal tax-exempt 501(c)(3) status is not yet confirmed for {{ORG_NAME}}. Until that registration is finalized and verifiable through the IRS Tax-Exempt Organization Search, contributions are not tax-deductible and we do not represent them as such.

How gifts are used: direct outreach (meals, supplies, emergency support), event costs, and minimal operating overhead. Quarterly recaps are published on the transparency page.

Receipts: emailed automatically. Keep yours for your records.

Refunds (donations): if you made a contribution in error, contact us within 14 days and we'll process a full refund.

Recurring giving: you can pause or cancel any time from the receipt confirmation email or by reaching out via the contact page.

Refunds & returns (shop)

Unworn, unwashed {{APPAREL_BRAND}} items can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Custom or final-sale items are non-refundable unless damaged. Email us through the contact page to start a return.

Accessibility statement

We aim to meet WCAG 2.2 AA as a baseline. The site is built mobile-first, with keyboard navigation, semantic HTML, alt text on imagery, and respect for reduced-motion preferences.

If you encounter a barrier on the site, please let us know via the contact page and we'll fix it.

Changes to these policies

We may update these policies as the mission grows or our infrastructure changes. The current version is always the one published here, with the date below.
