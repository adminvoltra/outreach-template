Shipping & returns

The basics.

How fast does it ship?

Orders ship within 2–3 business days from {{STATE}}. Standard delivery is 3–7 business days within the US.

Returns?

Unworn, unwashed items can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Email us through the contact page to start a return.

International?

Currently shipping within the US. International is in the works — sign up for the newsletter to be notified when it launches.

Sizing

Tees and crewnecks run true to size. Hoodies run slightly oversized — size down for a fitted look. Detailed size guide on each product page.

How much of my purchase funds outreach?

Around 25–30% of net revenue from each sale funds direct outreach (meals, kits, emergency support). The rest covers production, shipping, and brand costs. Quarterly recaps published on the transparency page show the actual numbers.
