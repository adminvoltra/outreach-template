Mission & values

What we stand on.

01

People first, always.

Every program is judged by one question: did a real person feel seen, fed, helped, or loved today?

02

Faith made visible.

We don’t hide our convictions. We also don’t weaponize them. Help comes first; conversation follows naturally.

03

Honest about everything.

What we’ve raised, what we’ve spent, what we still don’t have figured out — all of it goes on the transparency page.

04

Built for volunteers.

If you want to serve, we want to make it easy. The work scales when more people show up.

05

Local before national.

{{STATE}} first. Roots before reach. We earn the right to grow by getting our own city right.

06

Dignity, not pity.

No one we serve becomes a content prop. Stories are shared with permission, or not at all.

Frequently asked

Questions people usually have.

Are you a registered 501(c)(3)?

Not yet. {{ORG_NAME}} is currently a founder-led outreach mission in development. We’re working through formal nonprofit registration and will publish our IRS status, EIN, and any state filings on the transparency page the moment they’re finalized. Until then, gifts are described as contributions, not tax-deductible donations.

Where does my contribution actually go?

Direct outreach: meals, hygiene kits, clothing, emergency support, transportation for people in crisis, and operating costs for events. Quarterly summaries are published on the transparency page so you can see the breakdown.

How is {{APPAREL_BRAND}} connected to the mission?

{{APPAREL_BRAND}} is {{FOUNDER_FIRST_NAME}}’s apparel brand. A portion of every sale funds outreach — and product is regularly given directly to people we meet on the street.

Can I volunteer if I’m not local to {{STATE}}?

Yes. We help supporters host care-kit drives, fundraising challenges, and outreach events in their own cities. Reach out via the contact page and we’ll send you a starter kit.

How can churches, businesses, or media partner with you?

Use the contact page and select “Partnerships” or “Media.” We respond within 3–5 business days.
