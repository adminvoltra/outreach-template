Prayer & Encouragement

Sometimes the most important help is sitting, listening, and praying with someone — no transaction attached.

Impact story

“I hadn’t eaten in two days.”

On a Saturday outreach in late winter, a man named Marcus walked up to the truck and asked if there was anything left. There was — a hot sandwich, a bottle of water, and a {{APPAREL_BRAND}} hoodie because he was shivering through a thin jacket.

He cried. The team prayed with him. He came back the following week. That’s the entire model. One person, one need, one moment at a time.
